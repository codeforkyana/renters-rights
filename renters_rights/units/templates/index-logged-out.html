Renter Haven

{% trans 'How we can help' %}

Moving Out

When you're ready to move out, we can help you write a letter to your landlord. We can also help you include pictures of the unit to show what it looked like when you left.
{% trans 'Create Letter' %}

Moving In

When you move in to an apartment, you should take pictures showing what the unit looked like when you moved in.

{% trans 'Who are we?' %}

We are Code for Kentuckiana, a group of volunteers who are trying to find ways to make the area a better place for citizens.

Our backgrounds include social work, community organizing, computer programming, law, and more. You can read more about our organization on our about page.

We built Renter Haven because we were told by our partners at the Kentucky Equal Justice Center and The Metropolitan Housing Coalition that an application like this one could be useful.
